# 🧩 Expert × Skill Combos

> Stop installing skills one by one. One role, one bundle: **how the expert thinks + what the skills do**.
> Every combo is tested on WorkBuddy / QoderWork / Claude.

[中文](README.md)

## The idea in 30 seconds

- **Expert (`EXPERT.md`)** — a role prompt: how a 5-year veteran of the job thinks and sequences the work.
- **Skill** — an executable capability the expert actually calls at a given step.
- **Combo** — the ready-to-use bundle: 1 expert + 2–4 paired skills, with the expert explicitly stating *when to call which skill*.
